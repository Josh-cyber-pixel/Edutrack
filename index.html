Everything you need

One system. Zero chaos.

Everything a school administrator needs — from the first day of term to the last report card — in a single clean interface.

Automated Report Cards

Enter CA and exam scores once. EduTrack calculates totals, assigns grades using your school's scale, and generates printable report cards instantly.

Fee Management

Create fee structures for tuition, exam fees, feeding, and PTA. Generate invoices, record payments, and track defaulters — all in one place.

Attendance + Live SMS

Mark attendance in under 2 minutes. Parents receive an instant SMS the moment their child is marked absent — no phone calls needed.

Student Records

Full student profiles with photos, guardian contacts, enrollment history, and medical notes. Everything you need, exactly where you expect it.

Timetable Builder

Build and publish your school timetable once. Teachers see their schedule, students see their classes, and changes update everywhere instantly.

Announcements & Messaging

Post school-wide announcements or send direct messages to parents and teachers. Keep everyone informed without leaving the platform.
